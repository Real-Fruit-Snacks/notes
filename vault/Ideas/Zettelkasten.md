# Zettelkasten

A *Zettelkasten* ("slip box") is a network of small, atomic notes that link to one
another. It grew out of Niklas Luhmann's paper card system.

## Atomicity

Each note holds **one** idea. Small notes are easier to link, reuse, and rediscover
later — see [[Second brain#Why links beat folders]].

## Linking

A note earns its keep through its links. A useful prompt: *"what does this remind me
of?"* Then link to it with `[[wikilinks]]`.
