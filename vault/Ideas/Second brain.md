# Second brain

A *second brain* is an external store for the things you'd otherwise forget. The
loop is simple: **capture** what catches your attention, **connect** it to what you
already know, then **create** something from those connections.

> [!note] Definition
> Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) is the practice of capturing, organizing, and
> retrieving your own notes so they compound over time.

Two methods I lean on:

- [[Zettelkasten]] — atomic, densely-linked notes.
- Daily notes, like [[2026-06-09]], as a capture inbox.

This idea underpins everything in [[Welcome]].

## Why links beat folders

Folders force one home per note. Links let a note live in many contexts at once —
which is exactly what [[Zettelkasten#Atomicity]] is about.
