# Pizza dough

A close cousin of [[Sourdough]] — same flour, less faff.

## Same-day version

Use commercial yeast when you don't have hours to spare:

```text
500 g  00 flour
325 g  water
  10 g salt
   3 g instant yeast
```

Mix, rest 2 hours at room temperature, divide into 4 balls, then stretch and bake
as hot as your oven goes.
