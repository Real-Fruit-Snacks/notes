# Sourdough

My basic loaf. Shares a starter and technique with [[Pizza dough]].

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Flour      | 500 g  |
| Water      | 350 g  |
| Starter    | 100 g  |
| Salt       | 10 g   |

## Method

1. Mix and rest 30 min (autolyse).
2. Stretch and fold every 30 min for 2 hours.
3. Bulk ferment until ~50% risen.
4. Shape, cold-proof overnight, bake at 240°C in a Dutch oven.

> [!tip] Hydration
> Higher hydration = more open crumb but stickier dough. Start at 70% and work up.

The same starter feeds my [[Pizza dough#Same-day version|quick pizza dough]].
